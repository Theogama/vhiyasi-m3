About Us

some things about my past...

Vhiyasi M3 Architects was founded by Tshepo Machaba, an architectural professional who's experienced in crafting and creating beautiful homes and working spaces. For years we've been working closely with different individuals, organisations and start-ups to create beautiful projects. . We develop our projects in detail from an idea to final realization in a specific form, paying a lot of attention to both visual and technical aesthetics. Architecture is a visual storytelling link between the past, present, and future. This is an exciting professional field to be part of, especially in an ever-changing world.

Tshepo Machaba

Founder & Director
